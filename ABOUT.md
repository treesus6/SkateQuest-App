## Our Story

Skateboarding isn't just a sport - it's a lifestyle, a culture, and for many, a lifeline. But the skateboarding community faces challenges:

- **Fragmented Information**: Skaters rely on word-of-mouth to find spots, missing hidden gems
- **Isolated Communities**: Local scenes don't connect with each other
- **Economic Barriers**: Many talented kids can't afford equipment
- **No Recognition**: Street skaters have no way to track progress or earn achievements

**SkateQuest was born to solve these problems.**

We started with a simple question: *What if there was one platform that connected every skater, mapped every spot, and helped kids who couldn't afford boards?*

## The Technology

SkateQuest is built as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** using modern technology:

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Maps**: Leaflet.js with OpenStreetMap data
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL) with real-time capabilities
- **Geographic**: PostGIS for advanced location queries
- **Storage**: Supabase Storage for videos and images
- **Authentication**: Supabase Auth with anonymous sign-in
- **Deployment**: Multi-platform (Netlify, Firebase Hosting)

**Why PWA?**
- Works on any device (iOS, Android, Desktop)
- No app store approval required
- Offline functionality
- Instant updates
- Smaller download size than native apps

## Charity Program: "Skate for All"

### How It Works:

1. **Purchase**: Users buy custom QR codes starting at $2
2. **Customize**: Add trick challenges, messages, and XP rewards
3. **Hide**: Print skateboard-shaped QR codes and hide them around town
4. **Find**: Other skaters scan codes, complete challenges, earn XP
5. **Impact**: 100% of proceeds buy skateboards for kids in need

### Transparency Commitment:

Every quarter, we publish:
- Total donations received
- Number of skateboards purchased and distributed
- Recipient community locations (anonymized)
- Photos and stories from recipient families (with permission)
- Complete financial breakdown

**Current Status**: Demo mode - Preparing for real fundraising launch Q1 2025
